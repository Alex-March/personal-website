Volodymyr Zelenskiy

The man standing up for Ukraine

Timeline of Zelenskyy's career so far:

  • 1978 - born in Kryvyi Rih, Ukraine (former USSR)
  • 1995 - joined the local team competing in the KVN comedy competition
  • 1997 - Zelenskiy headed the Kvartal 95 team
  • 2000 - graduated Kyiv National Economic University with a law degree
  • 2003 - Kvartal 95 started producing TV shows for Ukrainian television
  • 2008 - starred in his first feature film "Love in the Big City"
  • 2015 - starred in "Servant of the People", a TV series about a school teacher winning the presidential election
  • 2019 - elected as the President of Ukraine on April 21
  • 2022 - on February 24, Russia attacked, sending missiles into almost all large Ukrainian cities, while also trying to take control of the country by force
